welcome to vidhaya niketan samiti

we are expert in education last 56 year

Ours is a registered charitable Society dedicate d to the field of education & child development. It began its journey in Feb 1963, when Shri Jugal Kishore, the then Education Minister of UP & a disciple of Swami Janardan Ji Paramhans, laid Foundation stone of a school at Muni Ki Reti , Rishikesh. The School was recognized as High School in the year 1980 & as Intermediate College in the year 1987 by the Government.

Currently, the Society is operating 3 institutions of repute in the region :


Shri Purnanand Inter College

The School Building has a Unique Modern Architecture in pollution free environment with an ambience...


Shri Purnanand Inter College

Set up in 1963- Affiliated to Uttarakhand Board, Celebrated Golden Jubliee Anniversary on 17th Feb'13 ...
