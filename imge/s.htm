Welcome To Shri Purnanand Vidya Niketan Samiti

We are expert in education from last 56 years

Ours is a registered charitable Society dedicated to the field of education & child development. It began its journey in Feb 1963, when Shri Jugal Kishore, the then Education Minister of UP & a disciple of Swami Janardan Ji Paramhans, laid Foundation stone of a school at Muni Ki Reti , Rishikesh. The School was recognized as High School in the year 1980 & as Intermediate College in the year 1987 by the Government.

Currently, the Society is operating 3 institutions of repute in the region :

Shri Purnanand Public School

The School Building has a Unique Modern Architecture in pollution free environment with an ambience...

Shri Purnanand Inter College

Set up in 1963- Affiliated to Uttarakhand Board, Celebrated Golden Jubliee Anniversary on 17th Feb'13 ...

Swami Purnanand Degree College

Swami Purnanand Degree College of Technical Education affiliated to Sridev Suman Uttarakhand University...

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

A Bachelor of Commerce (baccalaureates commercii, abbreviated B.Com. or B.Comm.) is an undergraduate degree in commerce (or business). After completion of B.Com, you can apply in any organization for the post of accountant. Every company requires accountant to keep track the profit and loss of their company. One must have knowledge in areas like Basic Principles of Accountancy, Cost Accounting and Management Accounting to be an accountant. Other than accountant, there are many career options in this field.
